Stay Safe Online – Checklist (For College Students in India)
•	Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
•	Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.
•	Always check the sender’s email ID or message source before clicking on links.
•	Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into banking or important accounts.
•	Keep your apps, devices, and antivirus software updated regularly.
•	UPI Safety: Remember that you do NOT need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money.
•	UPI Safety: Do not approve unknown “collect requests” or scan random QR codes.
•	Be cautious while downloading apps—install only from trusted sources like official app stores.
•	Limit the personal information you share on social media platforms.
•	Log out from shared or public devices after use.
Reporting Cybercrime:
If you become a victim of cybercrime, immediately report it on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call the helpline number 1930 for quick assistance.
