Responsible Social Media Use Checklist for College Students
 Do’s
•	Do think before posting anything online. 
•	Do maintain a positive and professional online image. 
•	Do verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news. 
•	Do use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication for security. 
•	Do respect others’ opinions and communicate politely. 
 Don’ts
•	Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly. 
•	Don’t engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or online arguments. 
•	Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content. 
•	Don’t trust or click on suspicious links or unknown sources. 
•	Don’t overshare your location or daily activities.
